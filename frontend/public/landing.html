Frequently asked questions

What is a funding rate?

A funding rate is a periodic payment exchanged between longs and shorts on perpetual futures. A positive rate means shorts pay longs — so you can earn simply by holding the right side of the position.

How does funding arbitrage work?

Funding Finder finds pairs where the rate differs sharply between two exchanges (e.g. Binance vs Bybit) and shows the spread. By opening opposite positions on each venue you capture that spread while staying market-neutral — no directional bet.

Do I need to deposit to use it?

No. Scanning, arbitrage detection, and 1 AI idea per day are free. You only deposit on an exchange if you decide to open a position.

Are my exchange API keys safe?

Keys are encrypted at rest on the server and used read-first. Auto-execution only runs with your explicit permission.

How do I pay?

In USDT via Crypto Pay and NOWPayments. New users get a 7-day Pro trial with no charge. Annual billing saves 20%.
